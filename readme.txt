Easily enlarge the product image in CMS by moving the mouse over the product photo in the WooCommerce product list.

You can zoom the photo of each of all your Woo Commerce products in your admin panel.

This magnifying tool will allow you to see the difference between very similar products.

== Installation ==
Requires
Plugin: WooCommerce

Unzip and upload contents of the plugin to your /wp-content/plugins/ directory.
Activate plugin in WordPress Plugins Menu
Enlarge your WooCommerce product photos in WooCommerce Products Menu.
